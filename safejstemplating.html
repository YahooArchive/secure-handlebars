Secure Handlebars

We support Handlebars JavaScript Templating engine with our Context Parser and XSS Filters alone.

Getting Started

In order to make our solution usable, we apply our solution to one of the most popular JavaScript Templating engines Handlebars. We hook our solution as a pre-processing process of the original Handlebars workflow as shown below, we analyze the output contexts of the templates and rewrite the templates compatible to Handlebars supported syntax, and register/apply our context-aware XSS filters as Handlebars supported helpers during the data binding process. In this way, we integrate our work as a pre-processing step without affecting the original data flow of Handlebars, making the solution to be integrated easily.

Figure 1 - Secure Handlebars

On top of that, we have extended our solution on the Express framework as express-secure-handlebars npm package.

Handlebars.js is an extension to the Mustache templating language created by Chris Wanstrath.
